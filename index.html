CABLE

The Community Atmosphere Biosphere Land Exchange (CABLE) model is developed by a community of users under the CSIRO - MIT BSD license.

In particualar, CABLE is used as the land surface model for the ACCESS coupled climate model supported in Australia for the Australian research community.
